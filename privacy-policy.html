Privacy Policy for UniCalc

Last Updated: 17-11-2025

UniCalc (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application UniCalc.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

UniCalc does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII), including but not limited to: Name Email address Phone number Contacts Photos or media Location data User-generated content We do not require users to create an account or provide any personal details.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

The app may automatically collect non-personal, technical data to improve performance and stability. This may include: Device model and manufacturer Operating system version App usage statistics Anonymous analytics Crash reports This information cannot be used to identify you.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for the following purposes: Improving app functionality and performance Enhancing user experience Identifying and fixing bugs Analytics for improving features We never sell your data or use it for advertising profiling.

3. Third-Party Services

UniCalc may use trusted third-party services for analytics or crash reporting, such as: Google Play Services These third parties may collect non-personal data in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can read their privacy policy here:

  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

UniCalc does not collect or store personal data on external servers. All calculations, inputs, and usage remain local to your device. If anonymous analytics or crash logs are collected, they are securely processed by third-party providers that meet industry-standard security guidelines.

5. Children’s Privacy

UniCalc does not target children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately

6. Your Data Rights

Since UniCalc does not collect personal information: We do not store data that can be retrieved, edited, or deleted. You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the app from your device.

7. International Users

UniCalc is available globally. Regardless of where you use the app, your information (if any is collected) is processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
